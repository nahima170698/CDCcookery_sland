Ingredients:
  • 450g Red Meat
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 50ml Water
  • 2 Garlic Gloves
  • 50g Tomatoes
Steps:
  • Season the meat with salt and pepper
  • Sear eminced gloves and tomatoes in saucepan
  • Add water in saucepan
  • Add the meat in saucepan
  • Let simmer at low temperatir for 15 minutes
